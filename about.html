WHAT IS INTERNET TEMPLE?

INTERNET TEMPLE is a place to perform music online.

HOW DO I PERFORM AT INTERNET TEMPLE?

  1. Follow these instructions to start a Hangouts on Air
  2. Use the Links icon in the bottom right to copy the Video Embed link
  3. Paste it below and press Submit

To play sound directly into Google Hangouts, reroute your audio using VB-Cable (Windows) or Soundflower (Mac).
Try using screensharing for visuals.

You can also use YouTube streaming instead, which allows you to use streaming programs like OBS for more complicated visuals.
Just be sure to still use the video embed link.
