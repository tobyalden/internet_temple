WHAT IS INTERNET TEMPLE?

INTERNET TEMPLE is a place to perform music online.

HOW DO I PERFORM AT INTERNET TEMPLE?

  1. Start a Hangout on Air using the button above
  2. Use the Links icon in the bottom right to copy the Video Embed link
  3. Paste it below and press Submit

To play audio directly into Google Hangouts, reroute your desktop audio using VB-Cable (Windows) or Soundflower (Mac).
